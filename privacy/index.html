Privacy

Your message archive stays on your Mac

Message Archive Utility works with a local archive. It does not upload messages for import, search, or export.

What stays local

Your imported archive is stored locally on your Mac. The app uses that local archive for browsing, searching, and exports.

What the app imports

This beta imports messages from an unencrypted local iPhone backup stored on your Mac.

What the app does not upload

Messages are not uploaded for import, search, or export. The core workflow happens on your Mac.

Backups and exported files

You choose where to keep backups and exported files. If you move or share an export, that copy is outside the app's control.

Private beta support

Support details are still being prepared for private beta testers. Public support information will be added before a broader release.

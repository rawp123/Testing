Download

Home Basis Tracker is currently a local Mac beta.

The Mac app is the intended desktop experience. A browser version is also available for local use, but browser storage depends on the current browser profile.

Availability

Mac beta

Mac desktop app

Built for local records on macOS 11 and later. Records and document copies stay in app-managed local storage on your Mac.

Local browser

Browser version

Useful for local testing or lightweight use. Records are tied to the current browser profile, so backups matter.

Before you start

  • Home Basis Tracker does not upload your records.
  • Local storage is not a backup by itself.
  • Export full backups and keep them somewhere you trust.
  • Review classifications and summaries with your CPA.

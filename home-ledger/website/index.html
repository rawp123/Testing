Local-first by design

Home Basis Tracker does not create an account, upload records, sync to a cloud service, add telemetry, or call third-party APIs. Your records stay under your control.

  • No account required
  • No cloud sync or upload workflow
  • No telemetry or tracking code
  • No third-party APIs for your records

Documents stay organized

The Mac app copies attached files into app-managed local storage. The browser version stores records and attachments in the current browser profile.

  • CSV and print exports include record and document metadata
  • Full backups can include private record and attachment contents
  • Local storage is not a backup, so export one regularly

What it is not

Clear boundaries are part of the product. Home Basis Tracker is a recordkeeping tool for organizing homeowner records.

Not tax software

It does not calculate taxes, deductions, or basis.

Not legal advice

It does not provide legal or professional advice.

Not cloud storage

No cloud sync, no uploads, and no remote account.

Not an automated deduction calculator

You review, categorize, and discuss records with your CPA.

Is this tax software?

No. It is a recordkeeping app for organizing home improvement records for review with a CPA.

Does it upload my receipts?

No. The app is local-first and does not upload receipts, invoices, permits, photos, or notes.

Can I use it with my CPA?

Yes. You can export CSV files and printable summaries to support a clearer review conversation.

What happens if I delete local data?

Local records can be removed if app data or browser data is deleted. Export regular backups.

Does it work offline?

Yes. The app is designed around local records and does not need an account or cloud connection.

Good habits for your records

  • Add records while details are fresh.
  • Scan or save supporting documents as PDFs or images.
  • Back up your data regularly.
  • Keep records for as long as you own the property.

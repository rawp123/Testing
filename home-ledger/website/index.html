Is this tax software?

No. It keeps home improvement records together.

Can I attach receipts?

Yes. You can attach receipts, invoices, permits, photos, and notes to the right project or cost.

Can I export a summary?

Yes. You can export CSV files and printable packets.

Should I make backups?

Yes. Export regular backups and keep them with your home files.

What should I keep outside the app?

Keep your own copies of important receipts, invoices, permits, photos, and backup files.

Backup habits

  • Add receipts and notes while the details are fresh.
  • Scan or save supporting documents as PDFs or images.
  • Back up your data regularly.
  • Keep major project records for as long as you own the property.

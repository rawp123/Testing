How it works

Build a private home records binder one project at a time.

Home Basis Tracker keeps the record structure simple: add a property, group work into projects, add expense details, attach supporting documents, and export summaries when you need them.

1

Add a property

Start with the property record and basic details such as address, purchase date, purchase price, and notes.

2

Create projects

Group improvements such as kitchen remodels, roof work, HVAC, landscaping, windows, permits, or repairs.

3

Add expenses

Record the date, vendor, description, amount, category, classification, documentation status, and notes.

4

Attach documents

Keep receipts, invoices, permits, photos, and related documents connected to the record they support.

5

Export or back up

Create a CSV, print a CPA review summary, or save a full backup for long-term safekeeping.

Organized around real homeowner records

Every expense belongs to a property and can also belong to a project. That makes it easier to review one renovation, compare work across years, or pull a complete property summary later.

  • Property records for one or multiple homes.
  • Project history with contractor, category, dates, and notes.
  • Expense records with classification and documentation status.
  • Document metadata and optional local attachments.

Built for review, not advice

Home Basis Tracker prepares organized records and summaries. Your CPA reviews the records and provides professional guidance. The app does not decide eligibility or calculate basis.

Export when needed

Use exports to take your records outside the app for review or safekeeping. Keep backups somewhere you already trust for important personal records.

Backups

Keep a second copy of important home records.

Backups help preserve the project details, costs, notes, and attached files you have added to Home Ledger.

What a backup is for

A backup gives you a separate file you can keep with your other home records.

What to keep outside the app

Keep your own copies of major receipts, invoices, permits, photos, contracts, warranties, and backup files.

Backups are your responsibility

Export a new backup after large project updates, after adding many documents, or before making major edits.

Backup files can contain home details, vendor names, amounts, notes, receipts, invoices, photos, and document contents.

Backup checklist

  • Export a backup after major project updates.
  • Keep a second copy with important home files.
  • Review summaries before sharing them.
  • Keep original receipts and permits when they matter.
  • Replace old backups when your project file changes.

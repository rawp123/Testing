FAQ

Plain answers for a local records app.

Home Basis Tracker is intentionally narrow: it keeps home improvement records organized locally and helps you prepare summaries for review.

Is this tax software?

No. Home Basis Tracker is a recordkeeping tool. It does not calculate taxes, deductions, or basis.

Does it upload my receipts?

No. Receipts, invoices, permits, photos, notes, and records stay local to the app environment you use.

Can I use it with my CPA?

Yes. CSV exports and printable summaries are meant to make a CPA review conversation clearer.

What happens if I delete browser data?

The browser version stores data in the current browser profile. Clearing that data can remove records and attachments.

Are backups encrypted?

Backups are plaintext JSON files and can contain private record and attachment contents. Store them carefully.

Does it work offline?

Yes. The app is built around local records and does not require an account or cloud connection.

Can I attach files?

Yes. You can attach receipts, invoices, permits, photos, and other supporting documents.

Does it support multiple properties?

Yes. You can keep separate records for multiple properties.

Is there a Mac app?

Yes. The Mac app is the primary local desktop experience. Availability is listed on the download page.

Keep a backup rhythm

  • Export a full backup after large project updates.
  • Keep a second copy outside the app.
  • Review records while details are still fresh.

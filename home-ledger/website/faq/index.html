FAQ

Plain answers about organizing home project paperwork.

Home Basis Tracker is intentionally narrow: it keeps home project paperwork organized and helps you prepare summaries when needed.

Is this tax software?

No. Home Basis Tracker is an organization tool. It does not calculate taxes, deductions, or basis.

Can I attach receipts?

Yes. You can attach receipts, invoices, permits, photos, and notes to the right project or cost.

Can I export a summary?

Yes. CSV exports and printable summaries can help when you need to look things over or share them.

Should I make backups?

Yes. Export a backup after major project updates and keep a second copy with your important home files.

What can a backup include?

Backup files may include home details, notes, receipts, invoices, photos, and other files. The backup uses a .json file format.

What should I keep outside the app?

Keep your own copies of important receipts, invoices, permits, photos, contracts, warranties, and backup files.

Can I attach files?

Yes. You can attach receipts, invoices, permits, photos, and other supporting documents.

Does it support multiple properties?

Yes. You can keep separate home details, projects, costs, and files for multiple properties.

Where can I get it?

Keep a backup rhythm

  • Keep a second copy outside the app.
  • Add receipts and notes while the details are fresh.

Frequently asked questions

Is this a desktop app?

Yes. Car Care Log is a desktop app built around local records.

Do I need an account?

No. There is no account required.

Are my records uploaded?

No. Your records and attachments stay local on your device.

Does OCR happen in the cloud?

No. OCR is handled locally.

Does it look up service intervals?

No. Car Care Log does not include service interval lookup.

Can it tell me what service I need?

No. It helps you organize and review your records. It does not diagnose your vehicle or determine required service.

Does it connect to mechanics?

No. You add records manually and attach your own documents.

Can I export my data?

Yes. CSV export, printable summaries, local backup folder export, and local restore are included.

Can I use it for more than one car?

Yes. You can track multiple vehicles in separate records.

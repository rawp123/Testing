Message Archive Utility for Mac

Make an iPhone message archive you can browse, search, and export without uploading your messages.

  • Imports from an unencrypted local iPhone backup on your Mac.
  • Searches conversations by names, words, and phrases.
  • Exports PDF, Excel, and CSV files you choose where to save.

Import from a local backup

Start with an unencrypted iPhone backup saved on your Mac.

Search conversations

Browse threads and search old messages by names, words, and phrases.

Export useful copies

Export conversations, search results, date ranges, or the full archive as PDF, Excel, or CSV.

First import

Start with a local iPhone backup.

Create the backup in Finder. Then import the messages the app can read and search them locally.

  1. 1

    Back up your iPhone in Finder

    Connect your iPhone, open Finder, and create a local backup on your Mac.

  2. 2

    Leave encryption off for this import

    For the current beta, make sure "Encrypt local backup" is not selected.

  3. 3

    Import, search, and export

    Choose the backup in the app, then search conversations and export PDF, Excel, or CSV files.

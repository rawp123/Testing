Privacy basics

Your archive stays on your Mac.

Local only

The app imports from a local iPhone backup and keeps the archive on your Mac.

No cloud upload

Messages are not sent to a website for import, search, or export.

No account required

The app runs on your Mac without a web account or remote message inbox.

How it works

Back up, import, search, and export.

  1. 1

    Back up your iPhone on your Mac

    Create an unencrypted local iPhone backup before importing messages.

  2. 2

    Import messages from that backup

    Choose the backup on this Mac and let the app create a local archive.

  3. 3

    Search conversations and export files

    Browse conversations, search old messages, and export PDF, Excel, or CSV files.

Exports

What you can export.

PDF

Readable conversation-style files for saving or printing selected messages.

Excel

Workbooks for reviewing message details, search results, and summary tabs.

CSV

Simple spreadsheet-friendly files when you want a lightweight copy.

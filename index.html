Message Archive Utility

A private Mac app for ordinary iPhone users who want to save, search, understand, and export their text messages locally.

Check availability See how it works

Your messages stay on your Mac. Nothing is uploaded.

Local first

Your archive stays with you.

Private by design

The app works with message data stored on your computer and keeps the archive in your Mac user folder.

No account required

The core workflow is local. There is no web account, cloud inbox, or remote message review.

Plain-language exports

PDF, Excel, and CSV outputs are made for people to read, print, sort, and share when they choose.

How it works

From phone in hand to searchable archive.

Who it is for

People who need their messages organized and readable.

Message Archive Utility is for Mac users who want a calmer way to preserve personal, family, research, or project conversations without sending their messages to a website.

  • Families preserving important conversations
  • Researchers organizing interview or project texts
  • Professionals saving client or volunteer threads
  • Anyone who wants a searchable local copy

FAQ preview

A few basics before release.

Does it upload my messages?

No. The app is designed for local use on your Mac.

What can I export?

You can export the full archive, a conversation, search results, summary reports, and messages with a selected person.

Is the first release Mac-focused?

Yes. The first release is being prepared for Mac users.

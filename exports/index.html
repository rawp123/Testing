Ways people use exports

Choose the part of the archive you need.

Saving important conversations

Export one thread as a readable PDF so it is easy to keep with other personal files.

Organizing old messages

Use Excel or CSV when you want to sort, filter, or review a larger set of messages.

Family memories

Save selected conversations that matter to you, such as planning notes, milestones, or shared stories.

Personal records

Keep a local copy of messages connected to projects, appointments, volunteer work, or household details.

Search results

Search for a word or phrase, review the matches, and export just those results.

Selected conversations

Pick a conversation or person and export only the messages that belong to that view.
